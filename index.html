Rube Goldberg Machine: Energy

.-.-.-.-.-.-.-.

This Rube Goldberg Machine uses the concept of energy. What is energy? What forms does it take? How is it transformed? How is it transported? How do we perceive energy? Why is "energy" a problematic concept? Can we play with energy? We invite the participants to think and tinker with these and more questions revolving around energy and connect a module that tackles those questions in a digital-material way.
